Welcome Home

Beloved,

You didn’t just stumble here by chance—God led you.

Heaven is rejoicing, and so are we. For the Word says in Luke 15:10, “there is joy in the presence of the angels of God over one sinner who repents.” Today, all of heaven is praising God because of your yes.

You said yes to grace. Yes to forgiveness. Yes to Jesus. And that one yes has changed everything. Chains have been broken. A new name has been written. Your soul has come home.

This moment may have happened on a warm day at the Atlanta Ice Cream Festival or in the stillness of your own heart—but it is holy ground. Right here, right now, your life is being rewritten by the Author of salvation.

We want you to know that you’re not alone. Just as the early church in the book of Acts devoted themselves to prayer, to fellowship, to breaking bread, and to growing in the Spirit—we are here to walk with you. Not just in celebration, but in discipleship, in love, in truth, and in power.

God has a plan for your life that far exceeds what you’ve seen or imagined. Eyes have not seen, ears have not heard, neither has it entered into the heart of man the things God has prepared for you—because you love Him.

If you need help finding a church home in Atlanta, we’ve prepared a list for you below. There’s also a playlist to fill your spirit with praise, truth, and reminders of His love.

May this moment mark the beginning of a life on fire for Christ. May you grow deeper roots, hear His voice, walk in obedience, and overflow in joy. Welcome to the Kingdom.

With love,
Your brothers and sisters in Christ,
and the Faith & Flavors family
